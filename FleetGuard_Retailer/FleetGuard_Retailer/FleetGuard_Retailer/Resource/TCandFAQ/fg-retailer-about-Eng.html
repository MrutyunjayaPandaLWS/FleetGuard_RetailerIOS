You will earn Samriddhi RLP points on all Fleetguard & Filtrum products purchased by you

Calculation of points...

Points would be calculated based on calendar month.

Calculation of points will start from the date of enrolment (subject to submission of valid ID and address proof documents)

Fixed Base Points

Every part number will have its own Base points (Refer Annexure).

On every purchase you will earn fixed base points. Total quantity purchased in that month will be multiplied with respective fixed base points, to arrive at total fixed base points for the month.
